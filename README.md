# ONEKEY

A student volunteer organization website dedicated to making a positive impact through community service and educational initiatives.

## About

ONEKEY is a student-led volunteer organization that connects young people with meaningful opportunities to serve their communities. Our mission is to empower students to become active contributors to society through organized volunteer programs and educational outreach.

## Website Structure


**Home Page** - Introduction to ONEKEY with our mission statement, featured programs, and key statistics about our impact in the community.

**About Page** - Detailed information about our story, values, and the people behind ONEKEY. Learn about our journey and what drives us to make a difference.

**Contact Page** - Meet our team members and get in touch with us. Find information about joining our organization or partnering with us for community initiatives.

## Getting Involved

Interested in joining ONEKEY or learning more about our programs? Visit our contact page to connect with our team and discover how you can contribute to our mission of community service and student empowerment.
